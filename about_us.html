STEP 1 - Typical Applicant Pool

College applications are about more than just GPA, test scores, and activities, but these elements still hold significance. Universities typically expect students to meet certain academic and extracurricular standards. The further ahead from everyone who is applying to that university, the better your chances of acceptance. We refer to the amount of value you need to bring in order to be accepted at a university as the ‘Value Gap.’ This gap varies in size among applicants. The larger your gap, the more value you will need to demonstrate to the university to get accepted.

STEP 2 - Know Your Value

Let's think of value like a special ‘something’ that makes someone or something important or useful to others! Understanding your value is crucial when navigating the competitive landscape of college admissions. It's about recognizing your unique strengths, passions, and experiences that set you apart. This is the differentiating factor between acceptance and rejection. Once you’re in the Typical Applicant Pool, you must understand the unique blend of qualities, experiences, and aspirations that make you a valuable resource to a university.

Every student should be able to quickly and confidently list 4-5 reasons why they are an asset you a university, but even most high achievers cannot do this. This stage is all about expanding a student’s self awareness and opening their eyes to the very things that make them such an irreplaceable asset to life.

STEP 3 - University Fit

After these 2 steps, it’s now time to know how to build a college list, and here we have 2 main things to consider. 

STAGE 1 - Create a tangible list based on the Typical Applicant Pool

The ideal university list in general, covers 3 categories of choices: your ‘reach colleges’, ‘target’ colleges, and ‘safety’ colleges. Your reach colleges are universities that are difficult to get into or  a bit higher than your current merits, but still possible. Your ideal colleges are universities where the typical enrolled student matches your profile, and your safety colleges are universities where you are very likely to get in.

STAGE 2 - Match the university's value with your Personal Value Profile.

Your personal value is your biggest asset in college admissions, and understanding this concept will highly increase your chances. More selective universities all have unique “flavors” of value they look for and find the students who demonstrate it best. During this phase you will thoroughly research the value of each possible university and come out with a great list of best-fit universities!

This is the stage that we will take you from a typical applicant with a more “flat” profile into an irresistible student whose top universities can’t wait to admit.

STEP 4 - Value Credibility Project 

You cannot ignore the importance of showcasing your true value to universities. It's not just about telling them that you’re great; it's about showing them in tangible and impactful ways. That's where our focus on Value Credibility comes into play. 

Value credibility is more than just a catchphrase; it's a philosophy that drives every single aspect of our approach at College Mastermind. We believe in the power of action and in demonstrating value through tangible efforts, meaningful experiences, and challenging projects. Every applicant vies for attention, so you can’t just list a bunch of activities and hope that you’ll automatically get into the “yes” pile!

A Value Credibility Project is a transformative journey that adds a unique layer of personal and professional development to your profile. The projects serve three main purposes: highlight and develop strengths, overcome or minimize gaps in your profile, and pursue a genuine interest as you explore career potentials.

 STEP 5 - Articulate Your Value

Now for our final step of the College Mastermind Approach, when you present everything you’ve done!  In this final step, you will build your application in order to show your top colleges that you deserve a spot at their university. Every single part of the application will be considered, including the activities list, the awards list, the additional information section, the main essay, any supplemental essays, letters of recommendation, interviews, and the entire application. 

All the pieces together will carefully demonstrate your value, showing undeniable proof that you are the ideal student for that college.

This is going to be one of the most powerful personal experiences of your child’s life. The goal is not to just to get into college, but to expand their perspective to develop a deep and mature view of their value beyond activities, grades, test scores, and accomplishments.

So many people believe that college admissions revolves around luck, but this isn’t true!  Surprisingly, it revolves around value. Our approach will push your child to uncover and claim the type of value that universities can’t get enough of, positioning them ahead of the other applicants at their top choices. Your child’s time with College Mastermind will completely change the way they present themselves to colleges, while taking them on a journey of self-discovery and personal mastery.
